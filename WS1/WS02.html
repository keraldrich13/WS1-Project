Welcome to Irene's Little Store

Irene Natividad is 44 years old and the owner of Irene's Little Shop, which started in April 2024 and sells street foods and some accessories.

Although fairly new, she's proved that she is happy with what she's doing, selling affordable foods and necessities to the community and with her friendly demeanor, she is a favorite among students in the barangay.

Irene's Little Shop is a proved that you don't need a big capital to start a business, but you need to put your heart into it and dedication to do what you're doing.
